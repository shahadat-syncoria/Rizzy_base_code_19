Odoo Canada Post

Seamless connection between Odoo & Canada Post

Canada Post provides flexible shipping and logistic solutions across the globe, including Canada and the United States.


Odoo Canada Post Connector - developed by Syncoria - is a perfect tool for businesses using Odoo Enterprise and Canada Post for managing orders and shipping operations. The app will help you connect Canada Post with Odoo. It is designed to reduce your time and effort and gain maximum output from the system.


With the Odoo Canada Post Connector, you can set delivery methods while creating Sales Orders, or you can even display them on Odoo Website. The order information would be automatically sent to Canada Post from Odoo. Plus, the Shipping label, Order Tracking number, and Shipping Cost would be sent from Canada Post to Odoo. Then you can print the Shipping Label, Commercial Invoice and send the tracking link to the customer via email.
